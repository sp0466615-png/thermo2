Mini Blast Smelting Furnace
Efficient Lead Recovery System

We are a trusted manufacturer and supplier of Mini Blast Smelting Furnaces (Mandir Bhati / Shahi Bhati) in Uttar Pradesh, India. This furnace operates on the principle of a traditional blast furnace and is designed with a rectangular refractory brick chamber and a conical hood for efficient processing. It works on a continuous basis, where battery scrap is processed and lead is extracted using soft coke as both fuel and reducing agent. As the required temperature is achieved, molten lead flows from the bottom and is cast into ingots, while an air pollution control system ensures environmentally safer operation.
