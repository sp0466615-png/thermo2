Industrial Rotary Furnace Solution

Thermo Tech (India) is a trusted manufacturer of Rotary Smelting Furnaces designed for high efficiency, durability, and continuous industrial operation.

Product Overview

Rotary Furnace is a mechanized batch-type furnace used for lead smelting. It rotates at low speed (1–1.5 RPM) ensuring proper mixing of raw materials and chemical additives.

The furnace is heated using oil or gas burners and lined with high-quality refractory materials for long-lasting performance.

Production capacity ranges from 2 to 25 metric tons per day.

Charging

Raw materials and additives are fed into the furnace.

Heating & Rotation

Furnace rotates slowly while heating ensures uniform mixing.

Melting

Lead melts and chemical reactions remove impurities.

Discharge

Molten metal is discharged through tapping hole into moulds.

Low Fuel Consumption

Optimized heating system reduces energy usage.

High Efficiency

Produces more batches per shift with consistent output.

Durable Design

Refractory lining ensures long operational life.

Advanced Pollution Control Integration

Furnace is connected with Air Pollution Control System to safely exhaust smoke and gases ensuring eco-friendly operation.
