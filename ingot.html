Product Description

The Ingot Casting Conveyor operates on a continuous chain system where moulds are fixed on standard attachment chains. These chains rotate at a controlled slow speed using electric motors and reduction gearboxes.

Molten metal is poured at one end and automatically discharged as solidified ingots at the other end after proper cooling through water spray and natural cooling.
