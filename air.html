Air Pollution Control System

Advanced Emission Control Solution for Lead Smelting Plants

Product Overview

Thermo Tech (India) offers a complete range of modern Air Pollution Control Systems specially designed for lead smelting and recycling plants. Our systems efficiently capture airborne lead particles and harmful gases to ensure safe and eco-friendly operations.

The system includes Cyclonic Settling Chambers, Cooling Towers, Cyclones, Bag Filters, Wet Scrubbers, and Chimney Stack to control emissions effectively.

The captured dust is reused in the furnace, resulting in an additional 3% to 4% increase in profit through lead recovery.

How It Works

1

Smoke and gases are collected from furnace through suction blower.

2

Heavy particles are separated using cyclonic chambers.

3

Fine dust is filtered using bag house filters.

4

Gases are cleaned using wet scrubber and released via chimney.
