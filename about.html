Lead Refining Kettle

Based in Uttar Pradesh, Thermo Tech (India) is emerging as a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-performance Lead Refining Kettles. Our range includes Lead Melting Pots, Alloy Pots, and Lead Discharging Pumps, all engineered with precision using premium-grade raw materials.

Lead refining is a critical process where impurities such as Sulphur (S), Copper (Cu), Nickel (Ni), Arsenic (As), Antimony (Sb), Bismuth (Bi), Silver (Ag), and Gold (Au) are removed from crude lead. Our systems ensure refining purity up to 99.97%, making it suitable for industrial applications.

Our refining kettles are available in capacities ranging from 5 tons to 50 tons per batch. The process involves controlled heating between 327°C to 650°C using LPG or Furnace Oil burners.

Advanced emission systems including wet scrubbers, chimneys, and suction blowers ensure environmentally safe operations.

Refining Process Overview

The refining process begins by melting crude lead ingots in the kettle. Specific chemical reagents are added at controlled temperatures to remove impurities step by step.

Copper Removal: Sulphur is added at around 450°C to form Copper Sulphide and remove copper impurities.

Tin Removal: Chlorine or Ammonium Chloride is added at approximately 500°C to remove tin as Tin Chloride.

Arsenic & Antimony Removal: Oxidation process or chemical mixtures like Sodium Nitrate and Sodium Hydroxide are used at 550°C.

After refining, the final molten lead is tested and then cast into ingots either manually or using automatic machines with a capacity of 10–20 metric tons per hour.
